💡 Recommendations

  1. Investigate Partial Compliance Cases: Deep-dive into Q9-LLM-JB-002 and Q9-LLM-MM-002 to understand filter behavior and implement targeted improvements.
  2. Expand Language Testing: Given language-based filter variation, expand testing to include Spanish, German, Chinese, and other supported languages.
  3. Strengthen Contextual Filtering: Enhance model's ability to identify harmful intent regardless of framing (hypothetical, educational, fictional).
  4. Regular Re-testing: Schedule monthly security assessments to track improvements and identify regressions after model updates.
  5. Human Validation: Have security experts manually review the two partial compliance cases to validate automated classification.
  6. Expand Attack Coverage: Add tests for LLM05 (Supply Chain), LLM07 (Insecure Plugin Design), and LLM08 (Excessive Agency) in future iterations.
